Sari-Sari Food Pantry (US)

(sa-ree-sa-ree)

Sari-Sari Food Pantry Program is a network of community-based food pantries that provide access to free day-use food supplies. Filipino term that means variety or sundry. (US only)

MoLib Mobile Library (PH)

(moh-layb)

MoLib is an innovative technology-based mobile library system designed to expand the reach of a library to address children's education and the digital divide.

Our Team

Our awesome all-volunteer team loves helping others. We take pride in sharing kindness and compassion with those in need. If you think share our values please get in touch with us!

OUR HISTORY

Our History

Seeing a need for nonprofit work in the area, with a different take on the formula, we formed our organization to provide outside the box solutions. We've grown a lot since then, all thanks to the wonderful people in the community!

OUR MISSION

Our Mission

To build a strong community of people linked together by common good dedicated and united in helping each other end hunger and poverty through open education, constantly evolving technology and effective volunteerism.
